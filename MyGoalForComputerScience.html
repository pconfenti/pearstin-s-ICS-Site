Gain Understanding

My main goal for this class is to gain a more in-depth understanding of the concept of computer science and computers in general. As of now, my understanding of all things computer related is minimal at best. I hope that what I learn in this class can help broaden my horizons and that by the end of it I may have a basic understanding.

Currently, I have no other computer science related projects in the works. I attribute this to my lack of knowledge of softwares/other things I could use to make a project. Hopefully, as I continue to grasp more concepts, I will be able to begin projects of my own.
