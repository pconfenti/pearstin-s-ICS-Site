Computer and Information Systems Manager

The job of Computer and Information Systems Managers is to coordinate the computer related activites for an organization. Some of the duties of CIS Managers include:

  • Regulate network security
  • Manage installation/maintenance of hardware and software
  • Oversee equipment training
  • Determine computer needs
  • Look into system upgrades

    • CIS Management is a more open ended job type. It is not limited to one area of expertise. For that reason, CIS managers are needed in nearly every company that deals with computers. If i were to enter the field of computer science I would want to find a positon as a CIS manager because most of the responsibilities involve overseeing and solving problems with computers, not necessarily designing.
